OEM/ODM

Tonnie has been offering exceptional OEM and ODM services to customers worldwide since 1970.
Our formulation R&D teams, 2D & 3D packaging design teams, production teams, sourcing teams,
and in-house laboratory uphold the highest standard exceeding the needs of our customers.

Formula

Our formula R&D teams work with partners worldwide
to create the best formulations for color cosmetics,
skin care, and cleansing products. Our dosage forms
include powder, liquid, emulsion, oil, ointment, and solid.
We also offer customized formulas.

Packaging

Our 2D & 3D design teams and sourcing teams are
constantly developing the most sustainable materials,
cutting-edge finishing, and innovated decorations for
color cosmetics, skin care, and cleansing products.
Please contact us to know more about your customized
packaging.

Decoration

Fantasizing the most eye-catching products? Our

realize your ideas and concepts. Please contact us
